What We Believe

At Kuwadzana Apostolic Church in Africa, our faith is rooted in the foundational truths of the Bible. We believe in one God, eternally existing in three persons: Father, Son (Jesus Christ), and Holy Spirit.

We believe in the divine inspiration and authority of the Holy Scriptures as the infallible Word of God. We embrace the saving grace of Jesus Christ, His crucifixion, resurrection, and ascension, and His promised return.

Our doctrine emphasizes the importance of baptism by immersion, the indwelling of the Holy Spirit, divine healing, and a life dedicated to holiness and service. We are committed to living out the Great Commission, spreading the Gospel, and nurturing a vibrant community of believers.

Through worship, prayer, and fellowship, we strive to grow in our understanding of God's will and to demonstrate His love and power to the world around us.
